What's new in Scilab 5.3.2?

  • Various improvements in Scinotes (See full changelog for more details)
  • Xcos module skeleton now available and documentation updated
  • Compression of JPG export is configurable and updated to 0.95
  • Crash management for GNU/Linux and Mac OS X: No longer kills Scilab and now shows advanced debug information
  • Many bug fixes
